## Improved Marketing Copy for DeBadkamer.com

### Critical Issue: Price Credibility

Your current copy shows **€3.500 – €15.000** everywhere as the complete bathroom renovation range. The market research shows the actual average is **€8.000 – €25.000**, with a market average of **€14.000 – €18.500**. Your current range represents only materials/products, not full renovation cost. This undermines trust when visitors compare with other sources. I recommend adjusting to more realistic ranges.
